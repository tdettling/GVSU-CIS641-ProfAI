Project Overview

ProfAI is an innovative web application designed to harness generative AI in creating dynamic, customized content for faculty at Grand Valley State University. This web app invites faculty members to begin by generating a realistic avatar of themselves, providing a personal touch to their content. Once the avatar is created, ProfAI generates a script tailored to the faculty member’s chosen topic. After the faculty approves the script, the application produces a video featuring the avatar delivering the content, accompanied by an engaging slideshow. These videos, ranging from 2-5 minutes, are fully downloadable. Following the project’s official launch in 2025, the GVSU IT Innovation and Research team will conduct interviews with students who have viewed the videos to assess the perceived value and authenticity of the content, ensuring that the tool meets both educational and engagement goals.
